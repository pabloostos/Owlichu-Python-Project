Owlichu restrictions

  1. The input must consist of a single word with no spaces or
    special characters.

  2. The word must be in all lowercase letters.

  3. The word cannot be longer than 45 characters, which is the
    length of the longest word in the English dictionary,
    Pneumonoultramicroscopicsilicovolcanoconiosis. (thought this
    was going to be shorter hahaha)

  4. The word must not exist in the current English dictionary.
    Note that while some words may exist in other sources, such
    as technical or specialized dictionaries, they will only be
    checked against the Merriam-Webster's Collegiate API.

  5. A word is considered invalid if it already exists in the Owlichu
    database. In other words, a word can only be added to the
    database if it is unique and does not already exist in the database.
